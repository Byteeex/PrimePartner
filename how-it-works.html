How it works

Structured workflows for faster decisions.

PrimePartner runs two coordinated workflows: daily opportunity intelligence for SDVOSBs and weekly teaming coordination for primes.

Quality bar

Only high-fit opportunities, clear deadlines, and transparent risks.

  • Exact due date and submission method documented.
  • Fit rationale and potential disqualifiers included.
  • Coordination notes kept current for each pursuit.
Workflow 1

Opportunity to SDVOSB (daily)

Daily monitoring, triage, and one-page brief delivery.

Monitor sources

Track public solicitations, amendments, and relevant updates.

Triage fit

Filter by scope, NAICS/PSC, capacity, and timeline.

Create Opportunity Brief

Summarize scope, must-haves, due date, and bid/no-bid guidance.

Bid/no-bid request

Send to the right SDVOSB with a 24-hour response window.

Support if bidding

Coordinate questions, compliance checklist, and proposal calendar.

Workflow 2

Prime contractor teaming (weekly)

Targeted outreach, vetted partner kits, and coordination support.

Build prime list

Identify primes aligned with agency, NAICS, and scope.

Outreach and value statement

Reach BD/SBLO teams with specific, relevant SDVOSB matches.

Share Teaming Kits

Provide capability statements, past performance bullets, and constraints.

Confirm scope split

Align roles, responsibilities, and proposal timelines.

Support coordination

Track documents, staffing, and follow-through to submission.

Artifacts we deliver

Clear documentation for every pursuit.

OB

Opportunity Brief

One-page summary with scope, due date, must-haves, and fit notes.

PK

SDVOSB Partner Kit

Capability statement, performance bullets, and staffing availability.

TS

Teaming Summary

Scope split, timelines, and responsibilities in one document.
