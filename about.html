About PrimePartner

Independent GovCon opportunity intelligence.

PrimePartner is an independent consulting service focused on opportunity research, SDVOSB teaming coordination, and operational support for government contract pursuits.

Mission

Deliver clear, qualified opportunities and partner matches so SDVOSBs and primes move faster with fewer missed fits.

What we are

  • Opportunity intelligence and triage support.
  • Teaming coordination and partner matching.
  • Administrative and documentation assistance.

What we are not

  • A government agency or contracting authority.
  • A guarantee of awards or outcomes.
  • A source of improper influence or insider access.
Approach

Professional, direct, and compliance-aware.

We focus on speed, filtering, and clarity so teams can make better decisions with less noise. Our support is advisory and operational, not legal or contractual.

PrimePartner does not submit proposals or act on behalf of the government. All services are provided in a support capacity.
