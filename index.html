How it works

Two workflows, one shared goal: faster, better-fit pursuits.

We organize opportunity flow for SDVOSBs and provide a structured partner bench for primes.

Workflow 1

Opportunity to SDVOSB (daily)

  • Monitor public sources for new solicitations and amendments.
  • Triage fit by scope, NAICS/PSC, capacity, and timeline.
  • Deliver a one-page Opportunity Brief with bid/no-bid guidance.
See full workflow
Workflow 2

Prime contractor teaming (weekly)

  • Identify primes active in the right agency and NAICS lanes.
  • Introduce vetted SDVOSB partners with a clean Teaming Kit.
  • Support scope alignment, timelines, and coordination.
