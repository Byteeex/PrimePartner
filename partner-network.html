Partner network

Vetted SDVOSB partners, shared responsibly.

PrimePartner maintains a vetted SDVOSB partner bench for teaming, subcontracting, staffing, and niche capability coverage. No sensitive data is published publicly.

Data handling

Partner data is shared only for relevant pursuits with consent.

  • Capabilities and constraints are documented.
  • Contact details are shared only with approved primes.
  • No confidential or controlled data is stored publicly.
Vetting process

Structured intake and verification.

01

Capability review

Confirm core services, NAICS alignment, and past performance summaries.

02

Availability check

Document staffing capacity, location constraints, and response timelines.

03

Fit matching

Match SDVOSBs to pursuits based on scope, agency, and delivery needs.

What we store

Data kept in the partner bench.

  • Capability statements and past performance bullets.
  • NAICS/PSC coverage and service areas.
  • Constraints, staffing availability, and response speed.
  • Primary contact information for coordination.
Partner data is shared only when there is a clear pursuit fit and with consent from the SDVOSB.
